Pulp Fiction

I love Pulp Fiction because it features many great actors at the height of their careers. The film features great action, cinematography, character development, and scenery. My favorite scene is when Samuel L. Jackson's character has an epiphany near the end.
Apocalypto

Apocalypto is one of my favorite films because it captures such raw emotion in a way that feels very authentic. The movie portrays the horrific fates of the protagonists and ends in a very interesting and reflective way.
IP Man

I admire the Ip Man movie franchise because of the skillful action sequences and historical storytelling that never gets dull. Donnie Yen and Michelle Yeoh always put on a dazzling display of martial arts while also being great actors.
